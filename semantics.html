Famous Ethiopian Landmarks

Lalibela Rock-Hewn Churches

The ancient town of Lalibela is home to 11 monolithic churches carved out of solid rock in the 12th century. It is one of Ethiopia's most sacred sites.

Axum Obelisk

The Obelisk of Axum is a tall granite monument from the ancient Aksumite Kingdom, showcasing Ethiopia’s long and rich civilization.

Blue Nile Falls

Locally known as "Tis Abay" (Great Smoke), the Blue Nile Falls are one of Ethiopia's most stunning natural wonders, located near Bahir Dar.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Addis Ababa
  • Language: Amharic
  • Population: ~120 million
  • Currency: Ethiopian Birr (ETB)

Did You Know?

Ethiopia is one of the few African countries that was never colonized and is the origin of coffee!
